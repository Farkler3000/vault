Crafting & Modding

The vault has a fully functional crafting system that doesn't use crafting tables. To use it, simply put in the 2 components for a recipe and take out the crafted item. Some recipes will be unlocked by default, while others can be found through quests or other methods. In total there are 30 crafting recipes for armor, weapons, and miscellaneous items. The modding system is used for weapons and armor with the moddable tag. These items can be combined with other components to upgrade them in the modding machine. These items have 3 tiers, their base form, tier 1 and tier 2. Tier 2 items are very hard to get, but are better than most other weapons and armor in the game.


Components

There are a number of components used for crafting and modding in the vault. Components can be found by scavenging in the wasteland or recycling junk. Components include springs, circuitry, aluminum, etc.

About

The vault is a project made by TriNyte, Farkler_, DahabTheCreator, and Sandy. It is a fully functional fallout vault inspired by Fallout 4. This vault is the second one made in the project, with the first one mostly being made by TriNyte and Farkler_. TriNyte is the lead on the project, but all members have a lot of say.
